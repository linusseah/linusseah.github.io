About Linus

Hi, I'm Linus.

Born and raised in Singapore (but constantly on the move), I'm currently based in San Francisco.

I enjoy building and bringing ideas to market.

Most recently, I was at Mito Health (YC S24), where I worked on bringing personalized health to more people. Before that, I was the founding PM of InterOpera where I built a web3 carbon trading platform to make carbon trading more accessible. Earlier in my journey, at Lazada and GlaxoSmithKline, I worked on products that supported small business owners and improved access to healthcare products.

I am currently actively building AI use cases across different industries. Some projects that I have worked on include developing retrieval-augmented generation LLMs to support enterprise workflows in finance, and applying General World Models to physical AI embodiments.

I am interested in opportunities in Solutions Engineering, AI Product, and Growth-related roles.

Outside of work, I'm a proud parent to a Cavapoo and too many plants, happiest when I'm out on a trail or being a flâneur. I'm also on a quest to find the world's best burger.

If you think I can help, please feel free to reach out!
